## Working Method

_Principles I apply to deliver analysis, models, and AI systems that are rigorous, reproducible, actionable, and built to last._

- **Institutional AI** — building AI as a durable organizational capability, not one-off projects
- **AI governance by design** — model documentation (Model Cards), registry (MLflow), and handoff protocols from day one
- Structured PoC lifecycle: hypothesis → discovery → evaluation → technical closure criteria
- **Reproducibility** — everything (code, data pipelines, model configs) in version control
- Evidence-based decision making: analysis should answer a clear question, not just describe data
- **Data storytelling** — bridging technical findings and strategic decisions through clear visualization and narrative
- Cross-functional partnership: R&D, MLOps, Governance, and Analytics teams must speak the same language
- Center of Excellence (CoE) model for capability development: training, taxonomy standards, shared frameworks
- Adhere to ethical AI principles — fairness, transparency, and accountability in every model deployed
